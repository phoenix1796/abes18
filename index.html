What are we ?

The First International Conference on Advances in Business and Engineering for Sustainability (ABES-2018) is organized to address various issues to prosper the creation of sustainable environment in business and engineeringin future. The aim is to bring together worldwide leading researchers, developers, practitioners and educatorsinterested in advancing the state of the art business & engineering for exchanging knowledge that encompasses abroad range of disciplines among various distinct communities.

ABES 2018 will no doubt be proven to be exciting and educative.

Objectives & Outcomes

This conference is targeted to engineers, scientists, planners, resources managers,modelers, policy makers, government officers, consultants, NGOs and students.Even in an increasingly networked world of internet and satellite conferences, there is no substitute for personalinteraction—what Edward R. Murrow calls &quot;the last three feet of communication.&quot; It is individuals, not data streams,who must ultimately build the connections that in turn create lasting international research partnerships.

Share research and best practices with global professionals

Receive informal in-depth feedback through discussions

Networking opportunities with delegates

Scope

Conferences are especially important in adapting faculty members to theirchanging roles in initiating and setting the directions for curricular changes. This conference provides an opportunity for researchers, academicians, scientists and professional engineers topresent their work, publish their results, exchange ideas and network for scientific and industrial collaborations.
The emphasis will be on peer-reviewed papers with high technical quality. The selected papers will be published in the conference proceedings. The conference also provides an opportunity for software/hardware vendors as well as publishers to display their products.

Best quality faculty members are the backbone of any successful institution, which imparts the good quality of knowledge and education to the student.
