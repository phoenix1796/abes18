Scope

Conferences are especially important in adapting faculty members to their changing roles in initiating and setting the directions for curricular changes. This conference provides an opportunity for researchers, academicians, scientists and professional engineers topresent their work, publish their results, exchange ideas and network for scientific and industrial collaborations.
The emphasis will be on peer-reviewed papers with high technical quality. The selected papers will be published in the conference proceedings. The conference also provides an opportunity for software/hardware vendors as well as publishers to display their products.

Best quality faculty members are the backbone of any successful institution, which imparts the good quality of knowledge and education to the student.

About college

Started with a mission to create an ambience for a healthy teaching learning process; to nurture students; and infuse in them a passion to excel professionally. A pragmatic, dynamic, global, high quality, creative and communicative approach in education has been adopted towards building a pool of budding technocrats and managers.
At this temple of learning, the focus is on human values, academic excellence, and composite personality development programs for enhancing the potential of individual students, enriching human values, and upholding the Indian value system- all acting in unison as a vehicle for growth at the national and international level, thereby making it a “student driven” institute of higher learning.

ABES Engineering College is a manifestation of academic heritage with strong belief in “Guru-Shishya Parampara”.
