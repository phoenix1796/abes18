Themes

The conference aims to cover the following areas of business and engineering for sustainable growth but not limited to:

Mechanical and Production Engineering, Aerodynamics, , Applied Mechanics and Design, Automation,Biomechanics, Computational Fluid Dynamics, Computer aided engineering design, Concurrent Engineering,Condition monitoring, Design and Manufacturing, Energy and Thermo fluids, Energy conversion system, EnvironmentEngineering, Energy Management, Finite element analysis, Fluid Dynamics, Fuels and Combustion, GreenManufacturing, Heat and Mass Transfer, Heat exchangers, Heat Transfer, Industrial Engineering, Industrial Tribology,Instrumentation and Control, Internal Combustion Engines, Machine Design, Machinery Dynamics Manufacturing, Mechanical Engineering

Analog and Digital Electronics, Power Electronics, Electrical Machines & Drives, Instrumentation & ControlEngineering, Power Generation, Transmission and Distribution, Power System Operation & Control, Alternativeenergy Generation and its Efficient Utilization, Signals and Systems, Smart Grids Technologies & Applications, Softcomputing in engineering, Power quality, Electric Vehicles, Signal Processing, Embedded Systems, Communication

Leadership & Change Management, Organizational Development, Innovation & Creativity, (SHE) Safety, Health & Environment ,Total Quality Management & Total Productivity Management, Leveraging Human Capital, MarketingStrategies & Customer Satisfaction, Product Innovations, Digitalization, Portfolio Management, SustainingProfitability, Financial Stability in Changing Scenario, e-Governance, government policies, legal and taxation structure, Entrepreneurship, Business Incubation and Intellectual Property Rights.

Submission Instructions

  1. Original papers based on theoretical or experimental works related to the above mentioned sub themes aresolicited for presentation in the conference.
  2. The paper should begin with title, short abstract and a list of keywords. Simultaneous submissions (papersalready submitted to other conferences/journals) are not allowed.
  3. All authors must follow instructions as per Paper template .
  4. Please do not submit plagiarized papers.
  5. The total length of the paper must not exceed SIX A4 size pages including bibliography and appendices.
  6. All the papers must be submitted through on-line paper submission process only (Easy chair submissionsystem). Submit your paper using below given link.

Submission Information

  • All the manuscripts submitted to the conference will go through double blind peer review process.
  • The conference proceeding will have an ISBN number.

*All papers of the conference will be published in UGC Approved Journals

* Selected papers will be published in SCI/SCOPUS Indexed Journals.
