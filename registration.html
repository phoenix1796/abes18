For paper presenters

Following requirements are mandatory to include your paper in Conference Proceedings

  1. CAMERA READY paper.
  2. CAMERA READY Source File.
  3. COPYRIGHT form duly completed and signed.
  4. REGISTRATION form duly completed.
  5. Scanned copy of Demand draft/ E- receipt of Fund Transfer.

Authors are requested to follow paper submission link on Website.

The paper length should not exceed 6 pages. 

Please send us the PDF of the Camera Ready Copy generated by PDF eXpress only.

Please mail a zip file containing the following documents to abesconf@abes.ac.in

  • camera ready paper
  • Source file
  • Copy write form with paper id
  • Registration form
